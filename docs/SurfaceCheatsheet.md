# Tennis Surface Characteristics

## Hard Court
- **Speed**: Medium-fast
- **Favors**: All-around players, big servers
- **Examples**: Australian Open, US Open
- **Key Players**: Djokovic, Medvedev, Sinner

## Clay Court
- **Speed**: Slow
- **Favors**: Baseline grinders, defensive players
- **Examples**: French Open, Monte Carlo
- **Key Players**: Nadal, Alcaraz, Ruud

## Grass Court
- **Speed**: Fast
- **Favors**: Serve-and-volley, big servers
- **Examples**: Wimbledon
- **Key Players**: Federer (retired), Djokovic, Alcaraz

## Indoor hard
- No wind/sun; cleaner serving/returning.
- Slightly faster conditions; favors aggressive baseline + serve.

## Surface Transitions
- Hard to clay: 4-6 weeks adaptation
- Clay to grass: Challenging transition
- Grass to hard: Moderate difficulty

## Historical Stats
- Nadal on clay: 92%+ win rate
- Serve effectiveness increases: Grass > Hard > Clay
- Break point conversion: Clay > Hard > Grass
